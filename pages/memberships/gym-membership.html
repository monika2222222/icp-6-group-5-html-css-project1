Gym Memberships

Realistic Membership Based Around Peoples Work

______
We have a four-tier membership system that allows members an affordable and realistic membership based around their work life, social life and family life. Our 24 hour platinum membership gives unlimited access 24/7, so no excuses.

Our New Member App

______
If you are a member, and wish to see how busy the gym is - download the Fitness Plus Gym App available on the App store and Google Play Store. Here you can send us a direct message, have your exercise programme uploaded and see the attendance leaderboard for the gym for that month. You can also access your QR code to scan into the gym, (as required by law for track and trace) so your key fob isn’t necessary.
